Ping-Pong!

Enter a number to see a special string of Ping-Pongs divisible by 3 and 5.

Here's the deal:

  1. Ping-Pong will count up (or down! hint-hint) to your number. However:
  2. All multiples of 3 are changed to "ping".
  3. All multiples of 5 are changed to "pong".
  4. When these multiples align: Ping-Pong!

Have fun!
